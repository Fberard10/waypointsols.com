Who We Are

Waypoint Solutions LLC is an Albuquerque-based U.S. small business built to serve federal agencies with construction services, industrial supplies, and facility support. We combine agile small business responsiveness with finance-educated leadership, compliance-ready operations, and a deep network of vetted subcontractors across New Mexico and the Southwest. Founded by Federico Berard and Braden Cote, Waypoint brings professional financial acumen and hands-on construction oversight to every contract we take on.

  • Site preparation, layout, and remediation
