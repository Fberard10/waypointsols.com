How We Serve Federal Agencies

Waypoint Solutions connects federal agencies with vetted contractors and suppliers across four core service areas:

Construction Services

Post and pole, chain link, wood and wrought iron fencing. Federal land and National Forest construction. Site preparation, layout, and remediation. Environmentally sensitive and light-on-land site work.

Industrial & Facility Supplies

Heavy equipment and machinery parts sourcing. Industrial consumables and maintenance supplies. Janitorial and facility support for federal buildings. Rapid sourcing with a verified supplier network.

Contract & Project Management

Full prime contractor accountability and compliance oversight. Subcontractor sourcing, vetting, and field oversight. Active CO/COR liaison throughout performance. Davis-Bacon wage determination compliance.

Equipment Solutions

Heavy and light equipment procurement. Low-impact tracked equipment for sensitive federal sites. Equipment coordination for remote and rural federal locations. Vendor partnerships across New Mexico and the Southwest.

About Waypoint Solutions

Our Mission

Waypoint Solutions LLC is a U.S. small business dedicated to providing federal agencies with reliable access to construction services, industrial supplies, and facility support through strategic sourcing, verified contractor partnerships, and expert project management.
