About Waypoint Solutions

Waypoint Solutions is a U.S. small business dedicated to delivering critical supplies to federal agencies with speed, reliability, and compliance. We serve as a trusted strategic partner in the federal procurement ecosystem.

Founded by Federico Berard and Braden Cote, Waypoint Solutions combines rigorous financial discipline with strategic sourcing expertise. Both founders hold bachelor's degrees in finance and bring advanced education in business and cybersecurity. Since high school, we've developed a strong partnership grounded in shared values around logistics, investment strategy, and operational excellence. Our mission is straightforward: provide federal agencies with access to quality supplies across multiple categories through verified suppliers and streamlined sourcing processes.
